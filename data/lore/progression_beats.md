# Progression Beats v1 (Lore-First, No Hard Tutorials)

## Beat 0 — First Whisper
- Player finds an **Old Grave**.
- Inscription pool emphasizes: **open shapes**, **three parts**.
- Grave loot can include: Runestone Dust, Anchoring Salt, an Early Fragment.

## Beat 1 — The Safe Foundation
- Player finds a small **Continuist Workshop** ruin with a broken bench.
- Recipe fragments seed:
  - Runestone Block
  - Limiter Sigil (safe)
  - Quiet Alembic (safe)

## Beat 2 — First Useful Magic (Potions)
- Player crafts the **Quiet Alembic** and brews one potion (Fleetstep or Pyroskin).
- A warning fragment teaches: *effects must end safely*.

## Beat 3 — First Staff
- Player locates Veinstone Crystal (thin spot or loot).
- Crafts **Cinderbranch** or **Stormreed** (safe) using limiter logic.

## Beat 4 — Echo Temptation (Optional)
- Player finds an **Echo Shrine**.
- Learns an unstable variant fragment (works, causes controlled consequences).

## Beat 5 — Broken Waygate Hub
- Player discovers a ruined **Waygate Hub**.
- Learns the resonance idea: *two gates, one core*.

## Beat 6 — Rebuild the First Network
- Player builds two Waygates with the **same Resonance Core**.
- Fast travel unlocks. Stable version has minimal side effects.

## Beat 7 — First World Gate
- Player discovers a portal site and assembles a **Timed World Gate** to Ignivar (or Velis/Axiom).
- Learns: *arch, keystone, timed limiter*.

## Beat 8 — Late Mastery (Evergates)
- Player finds Veilkeeper seal chamber.
- Crafts **Continuum Regulator**.
- Can keep a portal open indefinitely and close it manually.
